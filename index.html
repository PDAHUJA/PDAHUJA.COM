Our Story

Wisdom That Stands the Test of Time

P D Ahuja & Co., Chartered Accountants, was established in 2024 in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, by CA. Prateek Ahuja (Managing Partner) and CA. Dharmendra Kumar Ahuja (Senior Partner), with a clear vision: to create a forward-looking professional practice anchored in a rich legacy of trust.

The firm operates under the aegis of Dharmendra Ahuja & Co., Chartered Accountant — a proprietorship firm of CA. Dharmendra Kumar Ahuja, who has been in continuous practice since 1987. This heritage of over 37 years forms the bedrock of our professional credibility.

Our tagline — "Blending the age old wisdom with AI enabled marketplace" — captures our philosophy: time-tested expertise amplified by modern, AI-enabled tools to deliver advisory that is both reliable and relevant.

Our Story

Wisdom That Stands the Test of Time

P D Ahuja & Co., Chartered Accountants, was established in 2024 in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, by CA. Prateek Ahuja and CA. Dharmendra Kumar Ahuja, with a clear vision: to create a forward-looking professional practice anchored in a rich legacy of trust.

The firm operates under the aegis of Dharmendra Ahuja & Co., Chartered Accountant — a proprietorship in continuous practice since 1987. This heritage of over 37 years forms the bedrock of our professional credibility.

Our tagline — "Blending the age old wisdom with AI enabled marketplace" — captures our philosophy: time-tested expertise amplified by modern, AI-enabled tools.
